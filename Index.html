Bio

Matthew Tyler Musto, more known as Blackbear, is a musician, singer, songwriter and record producer. He started out in a garage band in Florida, after release of their ep the band was signed to Leakmob Records. From there Blackbear dropped out of school in the 9th grade to continue his pursuit of music. After leaving his band he then moved to Atlanta to work with Ne-Yo a famous artist from the mid-late 2000's. In July of 2011, Matthew would finally take the name of Blackbear after the release of his first ep Year of the Blackbear. In 2012, Mutso co-wrote Justin Bieber's "Boyfriend" that became #2 on the Billboard Hot 100. Since then he has produced songs for artist such as MGK, G-Eazy, Ellie Goulding and many others. He has made 5 full length albums, a secondary project with artist Mike Posner and several ep's. And has been featured on over 15 songs in the last 6 years.
